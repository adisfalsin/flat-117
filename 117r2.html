01 Bathroom
Basin Waste Pipe
Resolved — No Charge

During the rip-out phase our team identified that the existing basin waste pipe had been incorrectly installed without sufficient gradient, meaning waste water would not drain properly under gravity. This has been fully rectified today by our plumbers and the waste now runs at the correct fall, complying with current building regulations.

No additional charge applies. This has been resolved as part of our ongoing site responsibility.

02 Electrical — TV Aerial & Sockets
Aerial Cable Renewal
Works Complete

During first-fix works our team tested all TV socket outlets throughout the flat. Neither socket — beneath the living room window nor the bedroom window — was found to be live. On lifting the bedroom floor, we discovered the aerial cable had been cut at some point in the past.

To restore a proper signal to both new TV positions, we have run a new high-grade coaxial cable from the entrance hatch through to both locations. This work has now been completed.
