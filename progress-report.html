02 Works Completed
General Strip-Out
Complete

Full rip-out of kitchen and bathroom areas has been completed. All existing units, appliances, bathroom suite, tiles, and associated fixtures have been removed and cleared from site. Both spaces are stripped back to shell and ready to receive new works.

Electrical First-Fix
Complete

Electrical investigations and first-fix works have been completed. All socket outlets and TV aerial positions were tested. Neither TV socket position was found to be live — investigation revealed the aerial cable had been cut at some point previously. A new high-grade coaxial cable has been run from the entrance hatch to both TV positions, restoring signal throughout.

Design & Specification
Complete

Kitchen finish options presented to client — Howdens Clerkenwell White in gloss and matt finishes. Bathroom tile specification confirmed as Regal Grey Matt 60×29.9cm (SKU 752966, Topps Tiles). Surface area calculations completed: floor approx. 4.41 m², walls approx. 10.12 m², total with 15% wastage allowance approx. 14.53 m².

Tile Estimate — Estimated tile cost including 15% wastage: approx. £958.49. Prices to be confirmed at point of order. Client approval required before proceeding.

04 Scope Changes & Variations
Wall Unit Heights
Scope Change

The originally specified wall unit height of 900mm cannot be supplied within the agreed Howdens Clerkenwell White range. Units have been revised to 720mm height. This remains a significant improvement on the original 600mm units in situ and does not affect the overall aesthetic of the installation.

Cooker Hood
Scope Change

The chimney-style cooker hood originally requested has been replaced with a built-in integrated hood. This avoids the need for boxing works to the kitchen flank wall, simplifies the installation programme, and delivers a cleaner kitchen finish in keeping with the overall specification.

Exposed Chimney Breast
Scope Change

The exposed chimney breast feature cannot be delivered within the agreed programme. Following investigation, the works required to achieve a satisfactory finish are outside the scope and timescale of the current contract. This item has been removed from the current programme.

Bulkhead Removal
Scope Change

The bulkhead cannot be removed within the agreed programme. On opening the void, exposed loose cabling was found running through the structure, with unfinished surfaces inside. Removal would require remediation works beyond the current scope. The bulkhead will be retained and made good.

Note — The exposed cabling found inside the bulkhead void has been made safe as part of first-fix works. No remediation to the bulkhead structure is planned at this stage.
