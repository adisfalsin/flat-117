Dear Mrs Burlingame

Please find below our proposed tap options for the bathroom basin at Flat 117. All options are chrome finish, water-saving, and specified for free-standing or vessel basins. We have included options from both GROHE and hansgrohe — both leading German manufacturers known for quality and longevity.

Please review the options and advise your preferred choice at your earliest convenience so we can proceed with ordering. The kitchen tap will be presented separately once the worktop and sink are confirmed from the fabricator.
