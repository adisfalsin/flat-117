Dear Mrs Burlingame

Please find below my latest update from site at Flat 117 for 18 March 2026. Significant progress has been made across all areas since the last report. Bathroom wall tiling is now complete, kitchen fit-out is advancing with appliances being positioned, first-fix plumbing is done, the re-wire is complete and a new fusebox has been fitted.

A particularly important milestone this week: the worktop and stone sink templates were taken on Monday and have been sent to production. The window double-glazing template has also been completed. The sink bowl, toilet and concealed cistern boxing have all arrived on site.

Complete
